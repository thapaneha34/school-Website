Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to start your journey at Texas International College.

1. Application

Complete the online application form with your personal and academic details.

2. Submit Documents

Submit your academic transcripts, identification, and other required documents.

3. Interview

Attend an interview to discuss your academic goals and aspirations.

4. Acceptance

Receive an acceptance letter once your application is approved.

5. Enrollment

Complete the enrollment process and pay the necessary fees.

6. Orientation

Attend orientation to get acquainted with the campus and your program.
