Our Programs

Empowering students with a world-class education.

Science

Since its inception, Texas has had Science faculty as one of its streams. It is a +2 level course that is linked with the NEB. Through both theoretical and practical study, Texas aids in the development of each student’s expertise in the appropriate field. Students interested in pursuing a career in technology or similar subjects should pursue a +2 in science. A +2 degree in science empowers a student to work in the fields of medicine, engineering, and other applied and theoretical natural science disciplines.

Management

Texas Int’l College, which has been running excellently under Texas Int’l Education Network (2010), is a dynamic educational institution with academic programs (+2 Level) in Science, Management, Law and Humanities with outstanding Board Results of more than 94%. Texas has been founded with a set of academicians and entrepreneurs to meet the rising demand for qualified and skilled manpower in the field of Science and Technology, Management along with Hotel Management and Humanities. Since its very inception, Texas remains as an invitation to learning by both theory and practice.

Humanities

Since its inception, Texas has provided Humanities professors as one of the streams. It is a +2 level course that is linked with the NEB. Through both theoretical and practical study, Texas aids in the development of each student’s expertise in the appropriate field. Students interested in anthropology, archaeology, classics, human history, ancient and modern linguistics, languages, and politics, literature, philosophy, religion, performing, and visual arts should pursue a +2 in Humanities.
